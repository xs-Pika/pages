Who We Are

PicoElectronics is a leading global distributor specializing in integrated circuits (ICs), semiconductors, and mission-critical electronic components. With 25+ years of industry expertise, we serve OEMs, contract manufacturers, and design engineers worldwide with unparalleled speed, precision, and technical support.

As a trusted global IC components distributor, our mission is to simplify supply chain complexity through reliable inventory access, competitive market pricing, and exceptional service. We ensure critical components reach the right place at the right time, every time.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional value as a global IC components distributor through reliable access to high-quality semiconductors and ICs, expert technical support, and innovative supply chain solutions. We build long-term partnerships based on trust, transparency, and mutual success.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted global IC components distributor, recognized for quality assurance, service excellence, and our ability to solve the industry's most complex sourcing challenges with precision and efficiency.
